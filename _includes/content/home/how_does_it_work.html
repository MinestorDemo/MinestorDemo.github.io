How does it work?

Reactn is a digital feedback system. It can collect customer opinions and experiences at known locations, through mobile devices and laptops.

Reactn has a very simple interface, meaning customers can use it intuitively. In effect, it is a digitised comment card. But it does not use a limited set of rote questions that may not reflect the customer’s real concerns. Instead, Reactn uses smart questioning to drill down to the root of an issue or concern.

The answers are then fed back immediately to the most relevant people in your organisation who can resolve the issue. Feedback can be given directly to the customer in real-time, informing them of progress and thanking them for their help in raising the issue.

Reactn is a cloud-based platform that is easy to manage and quick to deploy. It uses a client-side dashboard to allow your empowered employees to keep track of issues – assigning tickets by action and priority.
