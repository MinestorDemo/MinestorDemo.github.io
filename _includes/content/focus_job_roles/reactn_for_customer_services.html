Reactn for Customer Services

Customer service professionals are challenged by the need to deliver consistent experience , while informing other areas of the business on where best to make investments for maximum return. These challenges are heightened by the limited measures currently available to them: whether it be surveys, social media sentiment, or good old fashioned sales data.

By introducing Reactn to the knowledge process, customer services can actively work on day-to-day customer engagement, pulling resource from across different functional lines into the process . This breaks-down divisional silos and treats customers as individuals, whilst also aggregating data for better decision making.

Reactn enables you to treat complaints, feedback and ideas as a source for improvement. It also enables customer service and experience teams to focus investment where it matter most and measure the granular impact these investments have on business success.
