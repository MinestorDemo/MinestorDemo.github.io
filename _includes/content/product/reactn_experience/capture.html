Capture location relevant feedback

Once selected, Reactn guides the customer through a structured and branded user experience. The app uses familiar mobile touchscreen technology, and leads the customer through a series of questions designed to draw out full and accurate feedback on any specific issue they may have encountered.

Feedback can include star ratings, rankings or free-form text, also allowing cusomers to attach photos to illustrate the issue. Other topics can be predefined, covering areas such as cleanliness, punctuality and friendliness of staff.

Critically, Reactn is dynamic. It adapts and responds to the answers given, steering customers through the process and modifying the questions they see according to their answers, their location, the time of day, even the weather.

Reactn is also intuitive to use, filtered out unnecessary questions. This makes the feedback exercise is brief, encouraging completion. At the end the process, customers are asked for some basic information such as name and email, so they can receive a response.
