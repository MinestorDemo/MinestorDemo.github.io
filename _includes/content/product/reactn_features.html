Rather than a one-way stream of complaints and platitudes, Reactn enables a constant loop of customer insight and resolution. We achieve this with a set of key platform features:


Always on

Too often, customer service is set up for the convenience of the company, rather than of customers. Reactn is different. Continuous and always on, Reactn operates at the convenience of the customer. Feedback can be given 24 hours a day and directed according to the severity and urgency.

Simple and compelling

Reactn uses straightforward and familiar mobile app and web interfaces. It provides an easy to use and compelling way of reporting issues that helps customers get their point across in a natural and engaging way.

Location-specific

Reactn uses geotagging and mobile GPS technology as well as unique response codes to locate customers and issues. This adds context and depth to the feedback collection process. As a result, Reactn can help you react faster and more precisely to resolve issues quickly and effectively.

Meaningful feedback

Reactn uses both carefully structured questioning to collect actionable feedback. In addition, users can upload photo evidence of issues. Improving the quality and relevance of feedback helps your organisation respond more effectively, driving consumer satisfaction and a better informed business.

Directed feedback

Improved response

Reactn enables relevant employees and services teams to collect and respond to customers directly. It also enables businesses to aggregate and act on feedback, both operationally and strategically.

Record actions and resolutions

Reactn handles the entire process through a unique ticket-based system, managing workflow, time-to-response, priority and escalation in one easily accessible platform.

Visualise performance and outcomes

Reactn aggregates sentiment, feedback and location data as a single dashboard. It also enables decision makers to view outstanding resolutions and faults across the entire business.
