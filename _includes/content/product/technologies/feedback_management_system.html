Feedback Management System

Feedback and data capture tools are created and managed within the Reactn platform. These include the creation of questionnaires, specifying the criteria used for routing feedback, and decisions about which staff members receive it. It also includes the management of responses, data outputs and dashboards.

Methods of capturing feedback include open-text and photo/video capture tools, star ratings and ranking questions. Reactn provides single and multi-response selection tools, as well as floating questions that appear at specific times of day or in certain situations. These tools can be branded according to requirement and routing criteria.
