Customer Access

Reactn makes it simple for your customers to leave feedback.

Built with mobile users in mind, Reactn is avalible as a web-based service. For regular users, Android and iOS apps (free to download from app stores) provide additional functionality, making the user experience richer and easier. This includes the ability to store their personal details, speeding up the feedback process and managing company responses.

Geo-location features allow users to easily find relevant businesses closest to them. While organisations can also drive the feedback process by publicising unique codes for customers to use.

A range of wider access technologies are also available, including website integration, Wi-Fi login screens, and integration with existing applications and kiosk solutions.
