The wealth of technologies available to passengers, mean that information systems, travel planning, delay compensation and social media, are the tools of the modern traveller.

Yet despite this, customer feedback systems still rely on forms, email and customer service lines, with very few positioned to address immediate customer experience issues or passenger frustrations.

Target investment where it matters most

Reactn delivers an agile and reactive environment for transport providers looking to engage customers and address changing requirements. From constant location-based sentiment, to specific carriage or bus stop fault reporting, Reactn empowers passengers to record, photograph and suggest improvements on every aspect of their journey.

Not only does this create a “crowd-sourced” approach to tackling litter, graffiti, maintenance issues and faults, is also brings passengers into the process of improving railways, bus services and airports.
