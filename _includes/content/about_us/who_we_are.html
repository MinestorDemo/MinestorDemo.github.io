Who we are

To retain this pace and continue to exceed client expectations, MineStor has built a team of committed professionals, who also happen to be a nice bunch of people.

Julian Brett: CEO and Founder

Julian founded his first company at the age of 21 and has been providing research services ever since. Julian oversees the development and delivery of MineStor solutions, with emphasis on technology strategy, research and service maximization.

Rod Hills: Business Development Director

Rod is responsible for MineStor’s sales and business development. Having worked in B2B sales for 20 years, Rod knows that opportunities for revenue growth stem from delivering superior customer experience. Rod understands how to listen to our customers’ needs and requirements, and how to align customer strategies with both your employees and your business goals.

Nathan Budd: Commercial Director

Nathan is responsible for marketing, channel partnerships and implementation of client solutions. He has more than 15 years’ research and consulting experience in high-level strategic planning,market entry strategy, branding, go-to-market campaigns and sales strategy. Nathan holds an MBA from the University of Bath, an MA in Marketing and a BA in Communications.

Michael Peacock: CTO & Lead Developer

Michael built the Reactn platform from scratch. Previously the CTO of a technology investment company, Michael’s talent for translating and balancing business and consumer requirements have led to a system which appeals to the challenges and objectives of both. He has extensive experience of managing technology teams and complex projects, as well as the business sense to take them to market.

Michael holds a software engineering degree from the University of Durham and is a regular industry speaker at global events. He is also the author of a number of highly regarded books and manuals on the development and use of technology platforms for the real world.

Jack Wagstaffe: Developer

Jack was awarded his MCOMP from the University of Newcastle, and came to MineStor to work on software development. With experience in creating information touch screens for the University and having specialised in security, Jack has been able to applying his cutting-edge thinking to the developing of the Reactn ticketing system and dashboard tools.
